Here are five basic games we made:

  • Guess the Number: A game where the player has to guess a randomly generated number within a certain range.
  • Hangman: A word guessing game where players attempt to guess a hidden word by guessing individual letters.
  • Whack-a-Mole: A game where players try to click on randomly appearing moles as quickly as possible.
  • Tic-Tac-Toe: A two-player game where players take turns marking a grid with their symbol (X or O) in an attempt to get three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.
  • Rock-Paper-Scissors: A classic game where two players choose between rock, paper, or scissors, and the winner is determined based on the choices.
